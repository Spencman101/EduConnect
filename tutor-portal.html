How EduConnect Works for Students

1. Sign Up or Log In

Create an account or log in to get started. You’ll answer a few quick questions about your subject needs and availability so we can tailor the experience for you.

2. Find Your Match

Search for tutors using filters like subject, rating, or availability — or let our smart matching tool suggest the best options based on your preferences.

3. Explore Tutor Profiles

Check out tutor bios, qualifications, teaching styles, and real student reviews to find someone who fits your learning style.

4. Book a Session

Pick a time that works for you from your chosen tutor’s calendar and book a session instantly. All your upcoming sessions will appear on your dashboard.

5. Learn and Share Feedback

Join your tutoring session and start learning! Afterward, leave a rating and short review to help other students and improve the tutor matching system.
